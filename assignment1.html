4. Assignment1: Cube compressionÂ¶

Using the same technique that you learned in the tutorial, do the following:

  1. Change the boundary conditions on the lower face of the mesh. Try to fix all the nodes on the lower boundary in all directions (x,y, and z) and see how this influences the result.

  2. Change the problem to a dynamics problem by

    • changing the *parameter card

      Parameter Name Value
      endT 100
      dtout 10
      dtmin 0.1
      dtmax 10

    • changing the imass parameter in *control_implicit_dynamics card to 1.
